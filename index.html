    I breathe life into your brand with *bits, pixels, and a little strategy. Working somewhere between design and business development, I guide startups and organisations from idea to fruition to promotion.

    Your brand is precious and there’s an incredible amount of work that goes into crafting its marvel — so I value all the universes that go into your project. Having worked with nonprofits, developers, and managers, I respect your manifesto, performance, and strategic goals. By working with your team, I can use design to position your message for both users and investors.

    I worked on my first real brand to grow a local business when I was 13. Since then, I’ve flirted with everything from video production and art catalogues to pitch decks and mobile apps, and most recently, web design and development.

    Slowly, I’ve learned to appreciate both breadth and depth in effective design. For each new project, I think about the overarching strategy and the implicit user experience before placing a pixel or writing a line of code.
