    The ultimate ingredient is stardust — mine is made up of bits and pixels. With these tools, I guide identities, experiences, and narratives from idea to fruition to promotion.

    Diversity is essential, so I value the importance of visiting every universe of design. I worked on my first real brand to grow a local business when I was 13. Since then, I've flirted with everything from video production and art catalogues to pitch decks and mobile apps, and most recently, web design and development.

    Slowly, I've learned to appreciate both breadth and depth in effective design. For each new project, I think about the overarching strategy and the implicit user experience before placing a pixel or writing a line of code.

    As a business student in a lovely cohort, I’m learning more about how to convert ideas into business endeavours and build striking brands along the way. Offscreen, I may be shooting film or reading theoretical physics.
